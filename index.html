▲ BUY signal triggers
●RSI < 35 — strongly oversold, high buy weight (+30 pts)
●RSI 35–42 — moderately oversold (+18 pts)
●RSI 42–50 — mild oversold lean (+8 pts)
●Price within 1.5% above support — strong bounce zone (+20 pts)
●Price within 3% above support — approaching bounce zone (+10 pts)
●Positive MACD bias — upward momentum adds buy weight
●Positive options flow — bullish sentiment adds buy weight
●Price down >1% today — intraday pullback adds buy pressure (+10 pts)
●Price down >2% today — deeper pullback adds more (+8 pts)
▼ SELL signal triggers
●RSI > 70 — strongly overbought, high sell weight (+30 pts)
●RSI 62–70 — moderately overbought (+18 pts)
●RSI 55–62 — mild overbought lean (+8 pts)
●Price within 1.5% below resistance — strong rejection zone (+20 pts)
●Price within 3% below resistance — approaching rejection zone (+10 pts)
●Negative MACD bias — downward momentum adds sell weight
●Negative options flow — bearish sentiment adds sell weight
●Price up >1% today — intraday surge adds sell pressure (+10 pts)
●Price up >2% today — sharper surge adds more (+8 pts)

▼SELL fires when sell score > 65 AND sell exceeds buy by 15+ pts

↻ Reanalyzing All Holdings
Fetching 3 weeks of trading data and updating support, resistance, RSI & MACD for each ticker…
What it fetches
60 calendar days of OHLC candles from Yahoo Finance (same proxy the price ticker already uses), then uses only the last 21 trading days for calculations.
What it calculates
  • RSI (14-period) — computed from the actual daily close sequence using the Wilder smoothing method
  • Support levels — identified by finding 3-candle swing lows (pivot points where the low is lower than both neighbors), clustered within 1.5% tolerance, then the 3 nearest below current price are selected
  • Resistance levels — same method but for swing highs above current price
  • MACD bias — 12/26 EMA crossover normalized to a −1→+1 scale relative to price
  • Options bias proxy — compares volume-weighted bull vs bear candles over the last 5 days as a sentiment indicator
  • Volatility — standard deviation of daily returns
After each ticker completes, the progress bar turns green and shows the live RSI and number of levels found. The signal panel re-renders immediately with the updated indicators.
