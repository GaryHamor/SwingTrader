▼ SELL signal triggers
●RSI > 70 — strongly overbought, high sell weight (+30 pts)
●RSI 62–70 — moderately overbought (+18 pts)

●Price within 1.5% below resistance — strong rejection zone (+20 pts)
●Price within 3% below resistance — approaching rejection zone (+10 pts)

●Negative options flow — bearish sentiment adds sell weight

●Price up >2% today — sharper surge adds more (+8 pts)
